Our Story

At Thriving Family LLC, we believe that mental health is the foundation for a healthy, fulfilling life. Our practice was founded with the vision of creating a safe, compassionate space where individuals and families can find healing, hope, and strength.

We provide personalized, evidence-based care tailored to the needs of each client.

Our team specializes in psychiatric evaluations, medication management, therapy, and wellness support for individuals across all stages of life.

We don’t just treat symptoms—we empower individuals and families to thrive.

Founder’s Message

As a nurse and psychiatric mental health provider, I’ve seen firsthand how mental health impacts every part of life. I started Thriving Family LLC to create a safe, supportive space where individuals and families can find hope, healing, and strength.

It is my passion and privilege to walk alongside you on your journey to wellness.

Funmi, APRN, CRNP, PMHNP-BC
Founder & CEO
